------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 NOTE ON THE RUPEE SYMBOL
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The template loads inputenc/fontenc with the Latin Modern font, which does NOT
contain the Indian Rupee glyph. Throughout these drafts amounts are written as
"Rs." (e.g. Rs.~52,000) so they compile cleanly. If you want the actual symbol,
add to the preamble:  \usepackage{tipa}  or  \usepackage[india]{fontspec} (XeLaTeX),
then replace "Rs." with the symbol of your choice.
