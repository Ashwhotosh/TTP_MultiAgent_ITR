 5. module3_flow.png   (Chapter 3, "Module Three -- Compliance Agent")

A decision-tree style flowchart:

     "Schedule OS"  <- ONLY if bank INT credit OR AIS SFT-004 OR deduction
                       evidence  (highlight: "no blanket guessing")
    |
  END: "ITR form + required schedule set".

Add a callout box: "Signal-based inference -> precision 96.2%".

 6. module4_flow.png   (Chapter 3, "Module Four -- Critic Agent")
==============================================================================
A verification-loop flowchart:

  START: "Claims from Auditor / Optimizer / Compliance"
    |
  STEP 1: "FaithfulnessVerifier (NLI, keyword fallback)" checks each claim
          against (a) source documents and (b) PageIndex legal corpus.

              "Orchestrator re-runs that agent" -> loop back to STEP 1.
              Label the loop "max 3 iterations".
    |
  Callout: "Removing the Critic raises hallucination 6.8% -> 18.4%".
